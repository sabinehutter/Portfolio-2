Flavin Capstone Project

~Won First Prize at UMSI ExpoSition~

Working along side Dr. Bruce Palfey at the University of Michigan Medical School, the purpose of this project is to analyze protein-flavin interactions in accordance with their chemical reactivity. The three goals include: first, to create a method for analyzing the relationship between flavin interactions and chemical reactivity; second, to create a method for storage of analysis; and third, to structure project for longevity and hand-offs to future project managers.
