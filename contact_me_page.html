Project Overview

Create a structural database for flavoproteins from the Protein Data Bank and extract geometric information from each structure that defines flavin-protein interactions. Once the database is created, classify and encode the interactions according to chemical criteria of varying levels of sophistication, and detect clusters of interaction motifs.
